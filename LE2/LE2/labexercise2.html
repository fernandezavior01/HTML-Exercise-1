Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was paited in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and
today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musèe du Louvre, Paris
